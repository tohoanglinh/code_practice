------------------------------------------------
WHERE IS MY MOTORBIKE?
------------------------------------------------

- Beacon is put on your motorbike
- Use radar-screen to scan your motorbike
- User-authentication to prevent unauthorized access
- Continuously scanning until found in-range (10m-100m)
